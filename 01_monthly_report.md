# Angavu – Monthly Business Summary Report

## Purpose
This document defines the structure and contents of the **Angavu Monthly Business Summary Report**.  
The report is designed for Tanzanian small and medium businesses (VAT and non-VAT) to:
- Organize business records clearly
- Prepare for TRA reporting with confidence
- Share with accountants or business partners
- Track monthly business performance

The platform **does NOT submit data to TRA** and does **NOT provide official tax filings**. Angavu is a tool to make your business records clear and transparent.

---

## Report Structure

1. Cover Page  
2. Business Details  
3. Reporting Period Summary  
4. Sales Summary  
5. Expense Summary  
6. VAT Summary (VAT-registered businesses only)  
7. Profit Estimate  
8. Notes & Legal Disclaimers  

---

## 1. Cover Page

**Contents**
- Angavu logo
- Title: “Monthly Business Summary”
- Reporting Month and Year
- Date generated

**Example**
> Monthly Business Summary  
> Period: March 2026  
> Generated on: 2 April 2026

---

## 2. Business Details

**Fields**
- Business name  
- Business type  
- Location (optional)  
- TIN (optional)  
- VAT status:
  - VAT Registered
  - Not VAT Registered

Purpose: Identify the business clearly without forcing sensitive fields.

---

## 3. Reporting Period Summary

**Fields**
- Reporting period (Month + Year)
- Number of sales days recorded
- Number of expense entries
- Currency (default: TZS)

Purpose: Provide context and completeness indicators.

## Design Constraints (MVP)
- No charts or graphs  
- No TRA logos  
- No signatures  
- No automatic submission  

Angavu reports are designed to be **professional, trustworthy, and clear**.
