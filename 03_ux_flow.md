# Angavu – UX Flow (User Journey)

## Goal
Design a simple, fast, and friendly workflow for Tanzanian SMEs to:
- Record sales & expenses
- Generate monthly summaries
- Stay prepared for TRA

---

## High-Level User Journey

1. Sign Up  
2. Add Business  
3. Enter Sales & Expenses  
4. Monthly Review  
5. Generate & Share Report  

---

## Screens

### 1. Onboarding / Sign Up
- Phone number (OTP verification)  
- Name (optional)  
> Fast, zero-friction entry

---

### 2. Create Business
- Business Name  
- Business Type (dropdown)  
- VAT Status: VAT / Non-VAT  
- Optional: TIN, Location  

> Clear: *“You can add this later.”*

---

### 3. Home Dashboard
- Current month  
- Total sales & expenses  
- Estimated profit  
- Buttons: Add Sale, Add Expense, Monthly Report  

---

### 4. Add Sale
- Amount  
- Date (default today)  
- Optional: Payment Method  
> Target: <5 seconds per entry

---

### 5. Add Expense
- Amount  
- Category (predefined)  
- Date  
- VAT toggle if VAT-registered  

---

### 6. Monthly Review
- Sales total  
- Expense total  
- VAT estimate  
- Profit estimate  
> User can confirm before report generation

---

### 7. Generate Report
- Preview  
- Share: WhatsApp, Email, Download PDF  

---

### 8. Reports History
- List of past months  
- Status: Generated / Locked  

---

## UX Principles
- Numbers first, words second  
- Always show “Estimate” for VAT & profit  
- Month-focused workflow  
- Offline-first where possible  
- Minimal cognitive load  

---

## MVP Screen Count
8 screens total, fast to implement, easy to use.
